Frequently Asked Questions

What is Haven?

Haven is a dating and friendship app designed specifically for neurodivergent individuals. We provide a safe, understanding space to practice social skills and build meaningful connections.

How do voice calls work?

Haven features AI companions you can practice conversations with. Simply tap the call button on any companion's profile to start a voice conversation. The AI remembers your previous chats and adapts to your communication style.

Is my data private?

Yes, your privacy is our priority. Conversation data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iOS device to view or cancel your subscription.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Update to the latest version from the App Store. If issues persist, contact our support team.
